The Competitive Arena

The market is dominated by a duopoly, but recent acquisitions by "super-apps" like Zomato and Swiggy are reshaping the battlefield, moving from pure-play ticketing to integrated lifestyle ecosystems.

Live Events Market Share (FY23)

The live events space is a tight race, with BookMyShow and Paytm Insider (now part of Zomato's "District") commanding nearly the entire market.
